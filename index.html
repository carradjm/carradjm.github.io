About Me

When I was 8 years old I made my dad buy me a "Teach Yourself C++" book. I'm fairly certain the book was too heavy for my scrawny 8-year-old self to pick up, but I went to work at it. Sadly, the allure of video games and football won out over the allure of coding and eternal glory, but something about creating something out of nothing stuck with me. I dabbled throughout elementary school and high school, creating a few stray websites with friends here and there before taking an intro level CS 101 class my third-year in college. That was when it hit me. Coding was awesome. Coding was fun. I wanted to do nothing else but coding.

I continued my education when I began working full-time after college, using every online resource I could get my hands on. Eventually, after some help from some very key players, I got to where I am today! Feel free to take a look around my portfolio and Github and see what I've created! You can reach me at malcolm.carradine@gmail.com.

Thanks, and have a great day.

-- Malcolm
